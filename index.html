About Us

Nehru Arts and Science College also known as Nehru College, Kanhangad, is a post-graduate and graduate college in Kanhangad affiliated to the Kannur University. Collge has been accredited with "A" grade by NAAC in the third cycle. In the NIRF ranking, NASC has been ranked 95 among all the colleges in the country. College was established in the year 1968. NASC offers 10 under graduate programmes and 5 post graduate programmes and 1 Ph.D. programme. Facilities in the college includes a 2 storey library and a centralized computer center.
